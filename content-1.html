What is eCommerce integration?

Simply put, e-commerce integration is the accumulation, exchange, and coordination of data between your company’s e-commerce data management system and other back-end channels such as accounting, inventory, sales, ERP, CRM, and marketing. The data sharing usually needs an application programming interface (API integration). With so many e-commerce players putting their foot in the door, the competition is cutthroat.
